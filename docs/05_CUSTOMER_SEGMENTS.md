# Customer Segments

## Primary Segments (Phase 1 Focus)

### 1. Corporate Boards & Executive Teams
- **Need:** Governance training, crisis preparedness, fiduciary skill development
- **Budget holder:** Board Chair, CEO, Chief Risk Officer
- **Deal size:** $25,000–$100,000+ annually
- **Channel:** Direct outreach, board advisory firms, governance conferences

### 2. Leadership Development Firms
- **Need:** Scalable tools to supplement coaching and workshop programs
- **Budget holder:** Managing Partner, Head of Programs
- **Deal size:** $12,500–$50,000 (licensing for client use)
- **Channel:** Partnership model — they resell or embed Simvado in their programs

### 3. Government Agencies
- **Need:** Crisis response training, ethical decision-making, public accountability
- **Budget holder:** Training Director, Agency Head
- **Deal size:** $50,000–$200,000 (multi-department)
- **Channel:** Government procurement, RFP responses, pilot programs

## Secondary Segments (Phase 2+)

### 4. MBA & Executive Education Programs
- **Need:** Interactive case study replacement, experiential learning
- **Budget holder:** Dean, Program Director
- **Channel:** Academic partnerships, conference demos

### 5. Law Schools & Medical Schools
- **Need:** Ethical dilemma training, high-stakes decision practice
- **Channel:** Academic licensing, curriculum integration

### 6. Sports Organizations
- **Need:** Leadership training for coaches, executives, and athlete development
- **Channel:** League partnerships, sports management programs

## Buyer Personas

| Persona | Title | Motivation | Objection |
|---------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **The Champion** | L&D Director | Wants measurable training outcomes | "Can I prove ROI to the board?" |
| **The Gatekeeper** | CFO / Procurement | Needs cost justification | "How does this compare to existing LMS?" |
| **The Sponsor** | CEO / Board Chair | Wants cutting-edge governance prep | "Is this proven? Who else uses it?" |
| **The End User** | VP / Director | Wants engaging, relevant experience | "Is this actually useful or just a game?" |

## Acquisition Strategy

**Phase 1 — Direct & High-Touch**
1. Warm outreach to board advisory networks
2. Free demo simulation as lead magnet
3. Pilot program with 3–5 enterprise customers
4. Case study development from pilot results

**Phase 2 — Scalable Channels**
1. Content marketing (LinkedIn, governance publications)
2. Conference presence (NACD, World Economic Forum)
3. Partnership with leadership development firms
4. Inbound via free demo funnel

**Phase 3 — Platform Effects**
1. Studio creators bring their own audiences
2. Marketplace discovery and cross-selling
3. Referral program for enterprise customers
