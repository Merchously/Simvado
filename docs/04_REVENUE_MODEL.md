# Revenue Model

## Revenue Streams by Phase

### Phase 1 — Foundation

**Individual Professional Subscription**

| Tier | Price | Access |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free Demo | $0 | 1 simulation module (Act 1 of Boardroom Under Pressure) |
| Pro Monthly | $79/month | Full access to all published simulations |
| Pro Annual | $699/year | Full access + priority new releases |

The free demo is critical for top-of-funnel conversion. It gives prospects a taste of the cinematic simulation experience with no commitment.

**Enterprise Pilot Program**

| Package | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Pilot (up to 25 seats) | $12,500 | 90-day access, onboarding call, usage report |
| Standard (up to 100 seats) | $25,000/year | Full access, admin dashboard, quarterly reviews |
| Enterprise (100+ seats) | Custom pricing | SSO, custom reporting, dedicated support |

Per-seat pricing within enterprise: **$250–$500/user/year** depending on volume.

### Phase 2 — Enterprise Scale

**Enterprise Licensing (Full)**

| Component | Price |
|-----------|-------|
| Annual Organization License | $25,000–$100,000+ |
| Per-seat add-on | $500/user/year |
| Custom simulation development | $100,000–$500,000+ per project |
| Enterprise analytics add-on | $5,000/year |

### Phase 3 — Marketplace

**Creator Revenue Share**

| Party | Split |
|-------|-------|
| Studio Creator | 50% |
| Simvado Platform | 50% |

Marketplace simulations priced by creator, typically $29–$199 per simulation or bundled into subscription access.

**Platform Fees**
- Listing fee: Free
- Transaction processing: Handled via Stripe Connect
- Featured placement: Premium listing available for creators

## Payment Infrastructure

- **Processor:** Stripe (subscriptions, one-time, invoicing)
- **Enterprise invoicing:** Stripe Invoicing + manual for large contracts
- **Marketplace payouts:** Stripe Connect (Phase 3)
- **Billing portal:** Stripe Customer Portal embedded in app

## Key Metrics to Track

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Enterprise contract value (ACV)
- Free-to-paid conversion rate
- Simulation completion rate (engagement proxy)
- Net Revenue Retention (enterprise)
